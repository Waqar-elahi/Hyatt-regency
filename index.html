Hotel Location

Map and Directions

123 Luxury Avenue, Paradise City, PC 12345

Directions:

  • By Car: Take the Paradise City exit on Highway 1 and follow signs to Luxury Avenue.
  • By Taxi: Inform the driver to head to 123 Luxury Avenue, Paradise City.
  • By Public Transport: Take the Metro Line 5 to Paradise Central Station, then a 10-minute walk to the hotel.

Nearby Attractions

  • Paradise Beach - 5 minutes away
  • City Museum - 10 minutes away
  • Sunset Shopping Mall - 15 minutes away
  • Grand Theater - 20 minutes away

Area Guide

Explore the best of Paradise City with our recommendations:

  • Restaurants: Try "Ocean Breeze" for seafood or "Golden Wok" for Asian cuisine.
  • Activities: Visit the Paradise Botanical Gardens or take a sunset cruise.
  • Cultural Experiences: Attend a live performance at the Grand Theater or explore local art galleries.

Policies & Terms

Booking Terms & Conditions

Reservation Policy

All reservations require a valid credit card to guarantee booking. Full payment may be required at time of booking for special offers.

Cancellation Policy

  • Standard Rate: Cancel up to 48 hours prior to arrival for full refund
  • Non-Refundable Rate: No cancellation permitted
  • Group Bookings (5+ rooms): 7-day cancellation policy applies

Note: During peak seasons and special events, different cancellation policies may apply as specified at time of booking.

Payment Options

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), bank transfers (for advance bookings), and cash (upon arrival).

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy.

Information Collection

We collect information necessary for your reservation and stay, including name, contact details, payment information, and preferences.

Data Usage

  • To process reservations and provide services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate special offers (with your consent)
  • To improve our services

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Check-in/Check-out Policy

Standard Times

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM

Early Check-in/Late Check-out

Subject to availability and may require additional fee:

  • Early check-in from 12:00 PM: 50% of room rate
  • Late check-out until 2:00 PM: 50% of room rate
  • Late check-out until 6:00 PM: Full day rate

Tip: Contact our front desk in advance to request early check-in or late check-out options.

Safety & Cleanliness Protocols

Your health and safety remain our top priority. We follow the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene.

Enhanced Cleaning Measures

  • Hospital-grade disinfectants used throughout property
  • Increased frequency of cleaning in high-touch areas
  • UV sanitization for room keys and high-touch items
  • Electrostatic sprayers for thorough room disinfection

Guest Safety

  • Contactless check-in/check-out available
  • Hand sanitizer stations throughout property
  • Staff trained in COVID-19 safety protocols
  • 24/7 medical assistance available if needed

Flexible Booking

We offer flexible cancellation policies for guests affected by COVID-19 travel restrictions.
